Exercise 6a: Head and Tail

Rewrite {(1↑⍵)(1↓⍵)} in a neat way using X⊂Y

      HeadTail 'Hello'
┌─┬────┐
│H│ello│
└─┴────┘
      HeadTail ⍳4
┌─┬─────┐
│1│2 3 4│
└─┴─────┘
